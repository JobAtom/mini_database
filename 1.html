Steel

Strong steel, made stronger.

We source the highest-quality steel for our blades. To harden from its raw state, we alter the nanostructure of the raw steel by heating it to over 2,000°F, cool it to less than -100°F and reheat it again to 500°F. After this process, we finish it with a rust-preventing coating.
